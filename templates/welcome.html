What is eKYC?

eKYC stands for Electronic Know Your Customer. It's a quick and convenient way to verify your identity using digital methods.

Here's what you need to know:

  • Your camera will be turned on throughout the process for facial recognition.
  • Your screen will be recorded to ensure the integrity of the process.
  • Make sure you have your Aadhar Card and PAN Card ready for verification.
